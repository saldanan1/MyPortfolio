Skills Vest

Design

The Skills Vest is a product meant to combat one of the issues children with Cerebral Palsy face every day: putting on clothing. The Skills Vest is meant to break down the mechanics involved in buttons and zippers (pulling, pinching, holding and slipping through a small hole). Games are attached onto the vest through pegboard-like holes and are meant to help teach children these basic skills. The vest is easily customizable and interchangeable, allowing it to be catered to any child's needs. For more information and a presentation on the project, please see the link below.
