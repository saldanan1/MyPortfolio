Skills Vest

Design

The Skills Vest is a product meant to combat one of the issues children with Cerebral Palsey face every day: putting on clothing. The Skills Vest is meant to break down the skills involved in buttons and zippers (pulling, pinching, holding and slipping through a small hole). Games are attached onto the vest through holes and are meant to help teach children these basic skills. The vest is easily customizable and interchanable: allowing it to cater to any child's needs.
